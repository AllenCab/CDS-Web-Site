I was a "do it all" dealer.  Measure, design, sell, pick-up, deliver, and install.  If I knew then what I know now I would have focused on the next sale where the bulk of the profit is made and let someone else spend time on the details.

Our Services

We provide technical order and sales support for Plain & Fancy Cabinetry dealers in portions of CT, NJ, NY, and PA.  This support is paid for by your Sales Representative.


We also provide additional paid services such as pre-sales pricing, field dimensions, consultations, post-sale order writing, order proofreading, floor plans, elevations, technical support, delivery support, and post-installation consultations for problem solving and field services.  Let us help you get it right.
