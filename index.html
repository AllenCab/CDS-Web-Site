About CDS

A dedicated partner
in your corner.

Cabinet Dealer Services (CDS) was founded to give Plain & Fancy dealers the operational backbone they need to thrive. Whether you run a boutique design studio or a high-volume dealership, we handle the coordination so you can focus on your clients.

Based in Pequannock Township, NJ, we work closely with dealers throughout the Northeast to streamline processes, navigate the Plain & Fancy system, and deliver outcomes that keep clients coming back.
